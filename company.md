## INTRODUCTION PAGE - ABOUT US
SK Polymer, established in 2018, is a manufacturer of plastic packaging products 
for the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries.

containers, jars, caps, and customized packaging solutions, produced 
with strict quality control and hygienic manufacturing standards 
to meet industry requirements.

## MISSION
To deliver high-quality plastic packaging products with a strong commitment to on-time delivery, consistent quality, and customer satisfaction.
