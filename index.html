About Us

SK Polymer, established in 2018, is a manufacturer of plastic packaging products for the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries.

Led by Mr. Maulik Sojitra, the company specializes in high-quality bottles, containers, jars, caps, and customized packaging solutions, produced with strict quality control and hygienic manufacturing standards to meet industry requirements.

Our Vision

To achieve sustainable growth and become a preferred packaging partner in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industry.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality plastic packaging products with a strong commitment to on-time delivery, consistent quality, and customer satisfaction.
